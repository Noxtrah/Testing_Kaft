Normal Flow:

1.User logs in.
2.User selects an item.
3.User add item to the wish list.
4.User buys the item in the wish list.
5.User enters payment information.

Alternate flow:

1 A. User can't log in.
3 A. User didn't want to add item to the wishlist.
3 B. Item is out of stock.
4 A. User cancels purchase proccess.
5 A. User enters invalid payment information.
5 B. Card balance is unsufficient.
